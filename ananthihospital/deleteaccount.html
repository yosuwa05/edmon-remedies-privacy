Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. If you wish to permanently delete your Ananthi Multispeciality Hospital account, please follow the steps below:

  1. Log in to your Ananthi Multispeciality Hospital account.
  2. Go to Account Settings.
  3. Scroll down and click on Delete Account.
  4. Confirm your decision in the final confirmation popup.
  5. Your account and all related data will be permanently deleted.

If you have any active subscriptions, please cancel them before deleting your account to avoid future charges.

Important: Deleting your account will remove all your health records, appointment history, and saved preferences. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Need help? Contact our support team.
