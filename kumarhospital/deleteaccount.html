Delete Your Account – Kumar Orthopaedic Patient App

We’re sorry to see you go. If you wish to permanently delete your Kumar Orthopaedic Patient App account, please follow the steps below:

Steps to Request Account Deletion

  1. Open the Kumar Orthopaedic Patient App on your device.
  2. Log in to your account.
  3. Go to Profile Page.
  4. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account.
  5. Enter the OTP received in the registered mobile number. Confirm the deletion in the popup confirmation.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

  • Your profile information (name, email, contact number)
  • All appointment history
  • All health reports or uploaded medical data
  • Any saved preferences or settings

Data Retention Policy

Once your deletion request is processed, your data will be permanently deleted from our servers within 7 business days. We do not retain any of your information after that period.

Developer: Wenoxo Technologies

If you change your mind, account recovery is not possible after deletion is completed.
