Privacy Policy

Effective Date:

1. Introduction

Welcome to Kumar Orthopaedic Hospital. We are dedicated to protecting the privacy and security of your personal and health-related information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and share your data when you use our mobile application, website, or orthopaedic services. It also explains your rights under applicable data protection laws and our commitment to complying with Google Play Store policies. By using our app, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect, process, and store various types of personal and sensitive data to provide orthopaedic care and related services, categorized as follows:

  • Identity Data: First name, last name, date of birth, gender, patient ID, or similar identifiers.
  • Contact Data: Billing address, home address, email address, and telephone numbers.
  • Health Data: Medical history, orthopaedic conditions, surgical records, imaging results (e.g., X-rays, MRIs), treatment plans, prescriptions, and other health-related information necessary for your care.
  • Financial Data: Payment card details, insurance information, or other financial details for billing purposes.
  • Transaction Data: Details about payments, insurance claims, and services (e.g., consultations, surgeries) received from us.
  • Technical Data: Internet protocol (IP) address, login data, browser type and version, time zone, location, operating system, and device information used to access our app or website.
  • Profile Data: Username, password, appointment history, preferences, feedback, and survey responses in our app or patient portal.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our app, website, or services, including interactions with features like appointment booking or rehabilitation tracking.
  • Marketing and Communications Data: Preferences for receiving health-related updates, newsletters, or promotional materials (e.g., about orthopaedic wellness programs).
  • Sensitive Permissions Data: If our app uses permissions like location, camera, or health-related APIs (e.g., Health Connect for tracking mobility or recovery progress), we collect data such as location for finding nearby clinics or health data for rehabilitation monitoring, with your explicit consent.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal and health data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and manage orthopaedic care, including consultations, surgeries, and rehabilitation programs.
  • To process payments and insurance claims.
  • To communicate with you about appointments, test results, treatment plans, or recovery updates.
  • To improve our app, website, and services based on user feedback and usage patterns.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements, including healthcare laws.
  • To send health-related information or promotions (e.g., about joint health workshops), where you have opted in.
  • To enable app features, such as location-based services (e.g., finding nearby clinics) or health tracking (e.g., monitoring mobility), where you have granted permission.

We only collect data necessary for the app’s functionality, and we disclose the purpose of any sensitive permissions (e.g., location or health data) in the app before requesting access.

4. Sharing Your Information

We may share your data with third parties under the following circumstances, ensuring compliance with privacy laws:

  • Healthcare Providers: Orthopaedic specialists, radiologists, physical therapists, or laboratories involved in your care, with your consent.
  • Service Providers: Trusted third parties (e.g., IT, billing, or analytics services) who process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Insurance Companies: For claim processing, with your authorization.
  • Third-Party APIs/SDKs: If our app uses APIs (e.g., Health Connect for mobility tracking) or SDKs, data may be shared with these services as disclosed in the app, with your consent.
  • Legal Obligations: To comply with laws, regulations, or legal proceedings, such as reporting to health authorities.
  • Business Transfers: In case of a merger, acquisition, or sale of hospital assets, with safeguards to protect your data.

We ensure that any third-party data sharing is disclosed transparently in this policy and within the app.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including encryption, secure servers, and access controls, to protect your personal and health data from unauthorized access, loss, alteration, or disclosure. Our app complies with Google Play’s security requirements to safeguard sensitive user data.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, including providing orthopaedic care and complying with legal or regulatory requirements (e.g., healthcare record retention laws). If you create an account in our app, you may request its deletion, and we will remove your data unless required to retain it by law. To request account deletion, contact us using the details below.

7. Your Legal Rights

Under data protection laws, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal or health data.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Restriction: Request restriction of data processing in certain cases.
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data to another provider.
  • Objection: Object to processing for specific purposes, such as marketing.

To exercise these rights or request account deletion, contact us using the details below.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you by updating the policy on our website and within the app. You are encouraged to review this policy periodically.
