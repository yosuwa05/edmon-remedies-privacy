Privacy Policy

Effective Date:

1. Introduction

Welcome to Little Smile Kids & Family Hospital. We are committed to protecting your privacy and safeguarding your personal and health-related data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you interact with our hospital, website, or mobile application, and informs you of your privacy rights and legal protections.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect, use, store, and transfer different types of personal data about you, categorized as follows:

  • Identity Data: Includes first name, last name, date of birth, gender, and patient ID or similar identifier.
  • Contact Data: Includes billing address, home address, email address, and telephone numbers.
  • Health Data: Includes medical history, diagnoses, treatment plans, test results, prescriptions, and other health-related information.
  • Financial Data: Includes payment card details, insurance information, or other financial details for billing purposes.
  • Transaction Data: Includes details about payments to and from you and services you have received from us.
  • Technical Data: Includes internet protocol (IP) address, login data, browser type and version, time zone setting, location, operating system, and other technology on devices used to access our website or patient portal.
  • Profile Data: Includes your username and password for our patient portal, appointment history, preferences, feedback, and survey responses.
  • Usage Data: Includes information about how you use our website, patient portal, or services.
  • Marketing and Communications Data: Includes your preferences for receiving health-related updates, newsletters, or promotional materials from us.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To provide medical care, schedule appointments, and manage your treatment.
  • To process payments and insurance claims.
  • To manage your relationship with us, including communicating about appointments or services.
  • To improve our website, patient portal, services, and overall patient experience.
  • To comply with healthcare regulations and legal obligations.
  • To send you health-related information or updates, if you have opted in.

4. Sharing Your Information

We may share your personal data with third parties under the following circumstances:

  • With healthcare providers, laboratories, or pharmacies involved in your care, as necessary.
  • With service providers who assist in hospital operations, such as billing or IT services, under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • With insurance companies for claim processing, with your consent.
  • To comply with legal or regulatory obligations, such as reporting to health authorities or in connection with legal proceedings.
  • In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of hospital assets, with safeguards to protect your data.

5. Data Security

We have implemented robust security measures to protect your personal and health data from unauthorized access, loss, alteration, or disclosure. These measures include encryption, secure servers, and restricted access to sensitive information.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal and health data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including to provide medical care and comply with legal, accounting, or regulatory requirements. Health records may be retained as required by applicable healthcare laws.

7. Your Legal Rights

Under data protection laws, you have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to:

  • Request access to your personal and health data.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request erasure of your data, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Request restriction of processing or object to processing in certain cases.
  • Request data portability to transfer your data to another provider.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the updated policy on our website or through other communication channels.
